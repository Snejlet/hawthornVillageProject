If you are looking for short-term accommodation in Castlebar, Hawthorn Village have apartments for you to book at affordable rates. Whether you are coming to see the sights, visit friends, work or study, you can keep your costs down by staying in one of our comfortable, modern apartments. Two and four bedroom apartments are available.

Stay with us and enjoy the following features and services:

•Internet access
•Great location for touring around Mayo and Westport
•Telephone facilities in each apartment
•Access to gym as well as a recreation room with a pool table
•Free parking
•Furnished apartments with spacious living rooms and all rooms en-suite
•Self-service laundrette on-site
•On-site manager available should you require assistance
•CCTV cameras in operation

Kitchen/Living Room

Each apartment features a stylish galley kitchen and is fully fitted with modern kitchen appliances, and comes complete with utensils and crockery. The living room of each apartment is provided with comfortable furniture and a television.
All apartments on upper floors feature generous balconies.

Bedrooms

All bedrooms feature ample storage space and an en-suite bathroom. Each bedroom is supplied with a desk, lamps, built in wardrobes and a heater.

Accessibility

Hawthorn Village features a number of apartments which have been specially adapted for people with disabilities. For further details, please contact our office.


Rental Period

Our short stay apartments may be rented for a period of four weeks onwards. If you intend to stay for a shorter period, please see our holiday apartment page for information and prices: Holiday Apartments

Key Holders

If you are locked out of your apartment or lose your key outside of office hours, there are key holders on site 24/7. Contact numbers are posted on the doors to the front office.

Additional Costs

•€80 security deposit which will be refunded at the end of your stay. •The rental fee includes fair usage of Internet and electricity, refuse disposal facilities, television license and access to recreation facilities.
•There is an on-site laundrette service with token operated machines. Tokens may be purchased at the office or from a dispenser in the recreation room for €2.
•In the event that you must avail of the key holder service to access your apartment, please note that there is a €5 fee which may be paid directly to the key holder or the front office at a later date.
